Antibiotics: Pros, Cons and Dangers

Pros:

  • Overall decrease of U.S. drug overdose deaths between 2023 and 2024 (110,000 to about 80,400)
  • Antibiotics have saved countless of lives since their discovery in 1928 (first was penicillin).
Cons:
  • At least 30% of prescribed antibiotics are deemed unnecessary in the United States.
  • The rate of antibiotic-resistant bacteria is increasing rapidly (estimated 10M deaths annually by 2050).

What causes Medication Overdosing?

While there are many factors contributing to this global issue, some of the main factors include:

  • Medication Overuse
    • Taking medication more than required
  • Medication Misuse
    • Taking medication in a way not intended by the doctor
  • Lack of Patient Education
    • Contributes to the two points above
    • Misconceptions and lack of complete knowledge of antibiotic effects
    • Patient expectations
      • Can lead to inappropriate drug prescription

What’s the plan with MedFacts 101?

  • Every week, I’m posting one slideshow on Instagram discussing a topic relating to using medication
  • I’m aiming to help contribute to the lack of patient education, which ASPs (Antibiotic Stewardship Programs) have already started to begin doing.
