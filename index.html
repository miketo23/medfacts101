Antibiotics: Pros, Cons, and Dangers

Pros:

  • Effective treatment of bacterial infections
  • Significant reduction in mortality from infectious diseases

Cons:

  • At least 30% of prescribed antibiotics in the U.S. are unnecessary
  • Antibiotic resistance is rising rapidly worldwide

What Causes Medication Overuse?

Several factors contribute to antibiotic overuse and misuse:

  • Medication Overuse
    • Taking medication more frequently than prescribed
  • Medication Misuse
    • Using medication in ways not intended by a doctor
  • Lack of Patient Education
    • Misunderstanding how antibiotics work
    • Expecting antibiotics for viral illnesses
    • Patient pressure influencing prescriptions
