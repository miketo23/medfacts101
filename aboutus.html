Mission Statement

MedFacts 101 aims to address the gap in patient education by making medication-related information clear, accessible, and easy to understand for a broad audience.

About

My name is Michael Okyere, and I am a high school student based in New York City with a strong interest in medicine and public health.

MedFacts 101 was created to address ongoing challenges in healthcare education—particularly medication misuse, misunderstanding, and overuse—by providing reliable and digestible medical information.

Our Approach

MedFacts 101 delivers weekly educational content through blog posts and Instagram slideshows, focusing on improving understanding of medication use and promoting safer health practices.

The goal is to contribute to broader public health efforts, including those supported by Antibiotic Stewardship Programs (ASPs), by increasing awareness and encouraging informed decision-making.
